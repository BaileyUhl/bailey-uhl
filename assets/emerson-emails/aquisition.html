Emerson Reid Acquires Rogers Benefit Group
National Expansion With More Broker Solutions
We are pleased to announce that Emerson Reid has acquired Rogers Benefit Group Inc., a national employee benefits wholesale general agent based in Phoenix, Arizona. Founded in 1948, Rogers Benefit Group has 33 office locations across 18 states and employs nearly 300 employees. For more information on this acquisition, visit our promotional website at www.emersonrogers.com. For more information on Rogers Benefit Group, visit www.rogersbenefit.com.
As we unite, Emerson Reid and Rogers Benefit Group will become the largest wholesale employee benefits general agent in the United States. Our carrier representation is expanded across a national footprint, providing local market access and expertise necessary to serve you and your clients. Together we now have a team of over 650 employees across more than 40 offices in 22 states and manage approximately $10 billion of insured premium on behalf of our broker partners.
Both companies will continue to operate “business as usual” while we harmonize our operations and identify the best-in-class support tools for our brokers. Eventually we will operate as one company, under one brand – Emerson Rogers.
Our Future: Emerson Rogers
To learn more about our merger and how our companies will unite, please visit www.emersonrogers.com.
