Kinsley Construction

Kinsley Construction, a large Mid-Atlantic construction company, needed a new website to stay competitive with other firms in the region. After reviewing competitor sites, I created detailed wireframes and built a clean, modern, and user-friendly platform using WordPress, Elementor, and custom code. I also designed and maintain a separate Kinsley Construction careers site that mirrors the main site’s look to maintain consistency.


With Kinsley’s wide range of job opportunities and a distinct target audience, I designed and maintain a standalone careers website that aligns visually with the main site to preserve the brand identity.


Beyond the main and careers websites, I also developed and manage an internal Shopify store that makes it easy for employees to purchase branded Kinsley merchandise.


Behind the scenes, I enhance SEO by tagging images with detailed metadata, optimizing copy with strategic keywords, and utilizing analytics tools. I use MonsterInsights and Google Analytics to identify high-traffic pages, guide website improvements, and inform our social media strategy.


Skills Highlight: Wordpress, Elementor, HTML/CSS, SEO, Analytics, UX/UI, A/B Testing, Mailchimp
